About Me

I'm Evan Christians, a 20 year old tech enthusiast.

I came to life choices hoping to learn a skill I would be passionate about and could make a career out of.

My application process began with an online test. I was consequently invited to a one-day bootcamp to test my affinity for problem solving. I was then accepted for the Life Skills portion of the application process. I learned many things about myself and what I should expect from the coding course. My application was successful and I am now excited to be a part of the student body of the Life Choices/ CodeSpace Web Development Course.
